Tools

This is just info for the dev. No need to inundate the player with so much information as these in-depth guides to the deep mechanics of the game.


class Tool_Cut:
	levels: 10
		level 	can cut through (fine or fast jobs require 1 more level)
		1: 		thin rubber, membranes, leaves
		2: 		paper
		3: 		cordage, tarp, thin flesh, thin foliage, whittling plastic
		4: 		plastic, thick rubber, flesh, foliage, whittling wood
		5: 		thick foliage, thick flesh, whittling bone, fine plasticwork
		6: 		rope; leather; thick cloth; 
					* at this stage technically capable of cutting through riveted metal mail rings, but only in combat situations, not when used as a tool in crafting.
		7: 		thin metal
		8: 		metal wire, metal sheet
		9: 		cable, metal rings, thin chain
		10:		chain, whittling metal

class Tool_Saw:
	levels: 6
		level 	can saw through:
		1:      plastic, cordage
					* serrated knives have at least saw level 1.
					* metal knives have at least saw level 1.
		2: 		rope, thin wood, thick plastic
					* serrated metal knife or bone saw, etc.
		3: 		wood, weak metal, bone
					* metal saws
		4: 		logs, sheet metal, fine bone sawing
					* big metal saws
		5: 		thick metal
					* power saws only.
		6: 		stone.
					* power saws only.

class Tool_Hammer:
	levels: 7
		level 	can be used for:
		1: 		basic carpentry (driving nails), work crude plastic
		2: 		advanced carpentry, basic weaponsmithing, armorsmithing
		3: 		for fine wood, stoneworking, crude metalsmithing.
		4: 		metalsmithing, fine glass working, very fine wood, stoneworking
		5: 		fine metalsmithing, crude steelsmithing, crude gemsmithing
		6: 		fine steelsmithing, specialized metalsmithing, gemsmithing
		7: 		specialized smithing, fast hammering (+power hammers)

class Tool_Anvil:
