
examine:
	give user info about something which the player character already knows.
	
identify:
	actively attempt to identify something better by observing it closely
	The better you can sense it, the better you can identify it
	Vision -75% for 1 turn
	
passive identify:
	every turn you passively roll to identify any unidentified entities within your field of view; the better you can sense them, the better you can identify them.
	you passively identify things you are holding in your hand or touching.
